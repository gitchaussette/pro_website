Website for a restaurant

Jonathan Roux created this project for a client, which owns an Italian restaurant.

His objective : Gain more visibility for the restaurant and display the pizza menu to the users.

His client is not very at ease with technologies, but he designed a specific administration interface for him, so he has the possibility to update his restaurant easily.

The project is simple but efficient, and gave entire satisfaction to his client.

I then followed his course on how to build that project to practice coding.

Technologies : HTML, CSS, DJANGO, PYTHON, HEROKU
